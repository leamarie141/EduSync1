About EduSync

EduSync is a user-friendly Learning Management System (LMS) created to help students organize and manage their school work in one place. It brings together all the essential tools you need to succeed academically.

"It allows users to manage a Track List with due dates, visualize workloads through a Schedule Viewer, utilized an integrated Study Time for focused sessions, and monitor performance via Simple Progress tracking — all within a simple, accessible platform designed for modern student life."
